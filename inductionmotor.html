Induction Motor

ATHO Induction motors are high efficient and suitable for continuous performance. Dynamically balanced rotor ensures vibration free and noise free operations. The varnish impregnated windings are made of high-grade enamelled copper wire. Shaft is made of high quality steel, precision ground of ample size for transmitting the rated horsepower.

Construction of motor frames and usage of quality materials result in high performance and low temperature rise, thereby increasing the life cycle of the motor. Frame and end shield are of high grade cast iron machined at close limits to ensure for an accurate alignment of bearings. In foot mounted motors, the frame and Legs are one single moulded piece that ensures resistance against vibrations. High quality, heavy duty bearings are used. All single and three phase motors require adequate motor protection control panel.
