Borewell

We are offering Borewell Submersible Pumps that features corrosion - resistant graded cast iron motor housing for more protection. Sturdily constructed, our Borewell Submersible Pumps have special solder joint between wire and cable, epoxy coated rotor to protect stamping against corrosion and dynamically balanced impellers and rotors. Borewell Submersible Pumps, offered by us, has special magnet provision for fighting against free metal content in motor.

ATHO added many varieties of such products such as electrical motor (SP & TP), V3, V4, V6 into their manufacturing list. We have more than 100 varieties of pumps & are proven to be reliable, durable & sturdy compared with other products in market. A perfect hydraulic design ensures higher efficiency by giving more discharge at lower power consumption. A product which is trouble free, gives excellent performance under vide voltage fluctuations, have good resistant against sand, performs round the clock at optimum capacity & is cost effective.
